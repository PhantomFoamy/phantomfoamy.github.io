Zombie Survival

Zombie Survival is a singleplayer wave survival game in which the player tries to survive as long as they can while purchasing upgrades in the shop to help them continue. The game is currently written by WappyCoder in scratch, but it is working on being converted to Phaser JS which should help it run better.


Controls:
W,A,S,D: Movement
P: Open Shop
E: Energy Shot
R: Roll
Space Bar: Chainsaw or RiotShield
1: Omega Bomb
2: Hydra Beam

Character Ability
Survivalist Gain 2 HP every 2 seconds. Revive yourself from death, with a 60 second recharge.
Banker Gain $2 every 2 seconds, and gain +$1 on every kill
Sacrificial Spirit Gain 1 HP and $1 every 4 seconds. Ability is upgradable, but higher upgrades increase Damage Taken.
Crate King Crate chance increased and gain a new, upgraded crate.
Flamethrower Replace Turret with Flamethrower
Gunner Fire Rate increased.
Heavy Ammo Bullet Damage increased.
Riot Shield Chainsaw Replaced with Riot Shield.
Tank Dash Removed, but gain 8 HP every 4 seconds. Bullet Damage incresed, but Movement Speed lowered.
Shotgun Replace Turret with Shotgun
Rocket Launcher Replce Turret with Rocket Launcher
Soldier Movement Speed increased and gain 1 Energy Ammo and 1 HP every 2 seconds.
